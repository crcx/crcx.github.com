Charles Childers

I am in my mid 20's, married, and have two children.

My employer is Denney Electric Supply. My primary roles there are purchasing and inventory management. I also assist in counter sales, web site maintenance, and various shipping/receiving roles as needed. My past employers include Target, A Beka Book, and Penndel Hardware

Programming is my favorite, but not only, hobby. I have a definite bias towards concatenative languages, and have been using various Forth dialects for the last nine years. Along the way I have become the primary developer of two concatenative languages (Retro; a Forth system, and Toka; an experimental attempt at building a more modern Forth for scripting purposes).

Most of the code I have written is intended specifically for my own use, or for jobs I have undertaken, but I have begun to share some small pieces via the forthlets project.

I do have one major project which is used by many people daily. This is Ent, a custom email autoresponder which fetches a variety of information from various sources, repackages it for display on mobile email clients (such as the Peek and BlackBerry). There are currently 50 services provided free of charge via Ent, and work is under way to open the data sources to other developers for use in their applications.
